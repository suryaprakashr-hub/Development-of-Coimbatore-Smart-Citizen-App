Railway Junction

Coimbatore Junction railway station, also known as Kovai Junction railway station (station code: CBE), is a major railway station located in the city of Coimbatore in the state of Tamil Nadu, India.

Coimbatore Junction is an important junction connecting major cities such as Chennai, Bangalore, Mumbai, Delhi, and Kolkata

It is one of the busiest railway stations in South India and serves as a gateway to the Nilgiri hills, a popular tourist destination.

It also serves as a gateway to several popular tourist destinations such as Ooty and Kodaikanal.Although Coimbatore railway station has the suffix Junction in its name, it's not a true junction
